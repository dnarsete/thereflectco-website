Our Brands

Two names. One standard.

Appose is our quiet-luxury brand. LipTX is the specialty line within it. Both are manufactured to The Reflect Co.'s proactive performance standard — the same in-house QC, sourcing discipline, and traceability across every run.

The Brand

Appose

Quiet luxury, tailored.

Appose is our quiet-luxury label — restrained, considered, made to last. Refined silhouettes in elevated fabrics, finished with the precision of a couture house and the discipline of a manufacturer's eye.

Where many brands chase trend, Appose pursues permanence. Every garment is built to be worn for years; every formulation is built to perform consistently from first use to last.

  • Considered design across apparel and accessories
  • Elevated, traceable fabrics and components
  • Built to last — finished and inspected in-house
Positioning

For the buyer who notices.

Appose isn't loud, and it isn't disposable. It's for the customer who recognizes restraint as a luxury in its own right — and the retailer who curates accordingly.

Within Appose

A focused line, fully integrated.

LipTX lives inside the Appose world. It carries the same standards, the same packaging language, and the same restraint — applied to a specific category.

An Appose Line

LipTX

The specialty line within Appose.

LipTX extends the Appose ethos into a focused product line. Manufactured to the same performance standard as everything we make, with the discretion and refinement of the Appose world.

Each item is batch-tested before release and traced through retail. No hidden inputs, no untested claims — just product that performs the way the label says it will.

  • Built within the Appose standard
  • Performance-grade formulation
  • Tested and verified, batch by batch
