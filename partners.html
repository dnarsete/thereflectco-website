Wholesale

A wholesale program with discipline.

Pricing, payment terms, and shipping are standardized so partners know what to expect on every order. No backroom discounting, no surprise terms.

01

Standardized pricing

Wholesale tiers are set centrally. Reps cannot manually discount — promotional pricing flows through approved promo codes only.

02

Clear shipping & tax

Default shipping is a flat rate. Multi-jurisdiction tax is handled at order time via Shopify Tax. Tax-exempt accounts apply once, then are auto-waived.

03

All sales final

No payment terms; payment at order. Returns are accepted only for shipping damage — documented and routed through your rep.
